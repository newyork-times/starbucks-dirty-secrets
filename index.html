Starbucks’ Dirty Secrets: Is your coffee made with Sewage Water ?

By Amanda J. Diaz

Amanda J. Diaz covers the Scandalls.

Jan. 20, 2025

A few days ago, I was scrolling through X (formerly Twitter) and I found a very interesting tweet made by Starbucks. They announced that starting in 2025, they’ll be using recycled water in stores. It includes making coffee, washing the dishes, and flushing the toilets.

At first sight, it seemed like good news. Until I saw another tweet from an NGO called Clean Water Alliance. They claimed to have conducted an independent investigation, and reported that Starbucks is lying and that they are endangering consumers' lives.

To be sure, I decided to contact the Clean Water Alliance to learn more about their investigation and what they actually discovered. I had a call with Richard Bowen, head of communication for the alliance. He explained all the process, details and founding of the investigation.

To begin, they ran several tests with a certified medical laboratory in different Starbucks stores throughout the USA, on different beverages proposed, including the best seller :
Strawberry Açaí Refresher, Magon Dragonfruit Refresher, Pineapple Passionfruit Lemonade Refresher, Iced Latte, Pumpkin Spiced Latte and Espresso shot.

They also ran similar tests on some dishes found in stores like bagel or cake pop. And the results were shocking : They found traces of many fecal bacterias and specifically one called “cassandromariusia virulentis” which is a very virulent and dangerous bacteria that could cause severe migraines and heart attacks.

Doctors expressed deep concern about this bacteria: “The discovery of this new bacteria is a breakthrough discovery for research, but can cause non-negligeable health issues. In most cases it’s only severe headaches, but for seniors and persons that have heart conditions, they can suffer from lightning heart attacks and die in front of the store.”

Indeed, Clear Water Alliance have drown up a list with all the names of people admitted in Emergency Rooms after going to Starbucks. They took the testimony of more than 10,000 people and they are preparing to sue Starbucks Incorporation for consumer endangerment, fraud, violation of Food Safety and Health Standards, false advertisement, environmental law violation and corporate misconduct.

With this trial consumers are trying to get compensation for their trip to the ER, but above all to draw attention and break the scandal to the whole world.

Taking a step back, the Starbucks tweet was posted on January 6th and the NGO tweet on 13th of January wich mean exactly a week after. This seems quite logical because running all those test need some times and we know that is was done with an professional laboratory. With all the informations that we got we can take a step back to understand the behind meaning. In fact, here the culprit is clearly Starbuks and the hero is our NGO. Without them we would never be able to see the truth and our children life could be in danger.

I am talking in the name of multiples colleague who decide create a petition so that the NGO could sue them. Beyond that, this article is putting on the light a national issue, the big firm we have are all intouchable and they allow themself to much right. We need to make a change by forcing them to respect human right.
